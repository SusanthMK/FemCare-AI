# Future Scope & Roadmap

## Vision

To evolve FemCare AI into a fully intelligent digital healthcare assistant with real-time predictive capabilities.

---

##  Short-Term Goals (0–3 Months)

* Improve UI/UX
* Collect user feedback
* Enhance basic tracking features

---

##  Mid-Term Goals (3–6 Months)

* Implement AI models (LSTM, ML models)
* Add personalized recommendations
* Improve prediction accuracy

---

## Long-Term Goals (6–12 Months)

* Integrate wearable devices
* Launch mobile application
* Expand user base

---

## Future Expansion (1+ Year)

* Global launch
* Multi-language support
* Integration with hospitals and clinics

---

## Advanced Features (Planned)

* Real-time hormone prediction
* AI-based fertility planning
* Mental health tracking
* Voice-based AI assistant

---

## Challenges to Address

* Data privacy concerns
* Regulatory approvals
* Model accuracy
* User engagement retention

---

## Final Goal

To build a scalable AI-powered platform that provides **continuous, personalized, and predictive healthcare support for women worldwide.**
